# 📊 Sales Forecasting & Revenue Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

The Sales Forecasting & Revenue Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze business sales performance, revenue trends, profitability, and regional performance.

This dashboard helps stakeholders identify high-performing products, profitable regions, and revenue patterns through dynamic visualizations and KPI tracking.

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance
- Track revenue and profit metrics
- Identify top-performing products
- Compare regional business performance
- Monitor monthly revenue trends
- Enable interactive business insights through slicers and filters
